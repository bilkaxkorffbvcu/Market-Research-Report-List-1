<p><p>The smart dash camera market is divided into two main types: single-channel and multi-channel systems. Single-channel dash cameras capture video from one lens, typically focused on the road ahead, making them a cost-effective option for basic vehicle monitoring. In contrast, multi-channel dash cameras use multiple lenses to provide extensive coverage, capturing footage from various angles, such as the front, rear, and cabin. This versatility enhances security and situational awareness, appealing to consumers seeking comprehensive vehicle surveillance.</p></p>
