<p><p>3D imaging in the smartphone market encompasses various components that enhance depth perception and object recognition. VCSEL (Vertical-Cavity Surface-Emitting Laser) provides efficient light sources for 3D sensing. Camera modules capture images in multiple dimensions, while narrow band filters help isolate specific wavelengths of light for improved imaging clarity. Lenses focus light effectively, and infrared receivers detect depth using invisible light. CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) sensors convert light into electronic signals, contributing to image capture, alongside other specialized technologies enhancing 3D functionalities.</p></p>
