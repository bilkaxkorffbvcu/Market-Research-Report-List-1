Corn starch, known for its thickening properties, is widely used in food and industrial applications. Potato starch offers high viscosity and is often used in sauces and bakery products. Cassava starch is valued for its gluten-free nature and is used in alternative food products. Wheat starch is typically utilized in processed foods. Other starches may include rice and tapioca starch, further diversifying applications across industries.</p></p>
