<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Radioisotope Battery Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermal Conversion Type</li><li>No-Thermal Conversion Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The radioisotope battery market comprises two main types: thermal conversion and non-thermal conversion batteries. Thermal conversion type batteries generate electricity by utilizing the heat emitted from radioactive decay, converting this thermal energy into electrical energy through thermoelectric generators. In contrast, non-thermal conversion type batteries directly convert gamma or beta emissions from radioisotopes into electrical energy, often using semiconductor materials. Both types are used in applications requiring long-lasting, reliable power sources, such as in space missions and remote sensors.</p></p>
