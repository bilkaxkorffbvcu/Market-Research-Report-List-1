<p><strong>The Oncology Companion Diagnostic Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR)</li><li>Next-Generation Sequencing (NGS)</li><li>Immunohistochemistry (IHC)</li><li>In situ hybridization (ISH)/Fluorescence in situ hybridization (FISH)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The oncology companion diagnostic market consists of several types: </p><p>1. **Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR)** is used to amplify DNA sequences, enabling the detection of specific genetic mutations linked to cancer therapies. </p><p>2. **Next-Generation Sequencing (NGS)** allows for comprehensive genomic profiling, identifying multiple mutations simultaneously for personalized treatment. </p><p>3. **Immunohistochemistry (IHC)** detects specific proteins in tissues, aiding in tumor characterization. </p><p>4. **In situ hybridization (ISH)/Fluorescence in situ hybridization (FISH)** visualize specific nucleic acid sequences within cells or tissue. </p><p>5. **Others** encompass additional methods like mass spectrometry and microarray analysis.</p></p>
